NUUC-Dispatch — Privacy Policy

Effective date: 2026-07-22

1. Data Collected

NUUC-Dispatch collects only the data necessary to verify your identity:

  • Google account email address and a stable account identifier (sub) — obtained via Google Sign-In (GIS), used to prove who you are to the requesting NUUC application.

NUUC-Dispatch does not read, scan, or access any document, spreadsheet, or Drive content. It performs identity verification only.

2. Data Storage

NUUC-Dispatch does not persist your identity beyond the verification request. No database or spreadsheet stores your sign-in.

3. Data Sharing

NUUC-Dispatch does not sell, share, or transmit your data to any third party. The verified identity (email, sub) is returned only to the calling page you signed in from.

4. Google API Scopes Used

  • openid, email — non-sensitive scopes, used solely to obtain and verify a Google ID token. No consent-screen review required.

5. Data Retention

No identity data is retained after the verification request completes.
